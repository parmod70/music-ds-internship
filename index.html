Data Science Internship Plan

Theme: Music (K-Pop or Current R&B)

Intern: Intern

Host: Coforge Data Science Team

🎯 Goals

  • Understand what data science is and how it's used in real companies
  • Learn basic Python programming and how to use Jupyter Notebook
  • Explore a dataset related to K-pop or R&B music
  • Complete a short data analysis project and share insights

🧠 Topic Options

  • K-pop Spotify Dataset: Analyze popularity, energy, danceability of recent K-pop songs
  • Current R&B Songs: Explore trends in tempo, valence, or artist popularity in the last 5 years
